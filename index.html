My daily Routine that helped me

  • I wake up everyday by 5am and then have my morning devotion.
  • After my morning devotion i read my sunday school book and then go for exercise.
  • After exercising my body, i write out my plan for the day: my schedule and how to carry them out.
  • Before and after writing my plan, i meditate and then finish up the planning process.
  • After all these steps i will then prep for the day, I start my day by 7am
